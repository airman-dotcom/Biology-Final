Homeostasis

How does the Respiratory System help maintain homeostasis?

It maintains homeostasis in the body by diffusing oxygen into red blood cells to transport all over the body, and disposing carbon dioxide waste.

How does the respiratory system interact with other organ systems?

    The respiratory interacts with a few systems:
  • The Circulatory System:
    • Together they transport oxygen to cells, and dispose CO2.
  • The Nervous System:
    • The respiratory system takes in the smell and the nervous system identifies it. Allowing us to be more aware in our surroundings.
